Placement Procedure and Policies

1). Institute’s training and placement cell contacts the companies and sends invitations along with germane information.
2). If any organisation is interested in placement and is willing to have PRE PLACEMENT TALKS, they shall put propositions to the institute along with the preferred period of time.
3). Based on the criteria and norms of organisation, the interested and qualified students will register for the recruitment process and/or interviews along with the required documents.
4). Training and placement team will assist the company in organising the tests or interviews on suitable periods and dates considering the students’ preferences as well as the company’s requirements.
5). After the completion of all the procedures and interviews, the organisation will have to announce the final list of placed students.
